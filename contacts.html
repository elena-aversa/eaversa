ELENA PROJECTS & RESEARCH CONTACTS

elenaaversa7@gmail.com, Information Designer, Post-Doc Researcher and Adjunct Professor at DensityDesign Lab, Polimi.

My name is Elena Aversa. I deeply enjoy conducting research, particularly when it involves information visualisation, digital media and information literacy (DMIL), digital methods, and audience-centred approaches to evaluating visualisations. I hold a PhD in Information Design, a Master’s degree in Communication Design, and a Bachelor’s degree in Architecture.

I’m passionate about teaching, both in academic settings and in collaboration with industry. My work spans information visualisation, graphic and editorial projects, as well as digital design. I also find joy in drawing, whether on paper or digitally using my iPad.

At present, I carry out research at DensityDesign Lab and teach at Politecnico di Milano, Poli.Design, and NABA. I also collaborate with the University of Amsterdam as part of the Digital Methods Initiative’s Summer and Winter Schools. Previously, I have held visiting researcher positions at Aarhus University, Roskilde University, and Aalto University.
